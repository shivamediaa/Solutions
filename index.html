Our Story

More Than An Agency.

Born in Hyderabad. Built on bold ideas. Driven by results. Shiva Media is the creative force transforming businesses across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and beyond.

We've spent years understanding the unique pulse of the Telugu business landscape — the aspirations, the culture, the competitive dynamics. That local intelligence, combined with global design sensibility, is what makes Shiva Media different.

We also offer full remote collaboration — serving clients across India and internationally, with the same quality and commitment as our local work.

Our UK Services

Everything You Need.

Complete end-to-end support — from your first consultation to your flight to the UK.

🎓
University Shortlisting

We analyse your academic profile, budget, career goals and preferences to shortlist the best-fit UK universities — saving you weeks of research and maximising your chances of success.

  • Profile evaluation & gap analysis
  • University ranking & reputation research
  • Course-to-career mapping
  • Budget-aligned recommendations
  • Russell Group & top-tier options
📋
Application Assistance

Our team prepares every element of your application — from compelling SOP and LOR guidance to academic document preparation — to give you the highest possible acceptance rate.

  • SOP (Statement of Purpose) writing
  • LOR (Letter of Recommendation) guidance
  • Academic document preparation
  • Application form submission
  • Interview preparation
📩
Offer Letter in 7 Days*

We know exactly how to fast-track university applications to get conditional or unconditional offer letters in as little as 7 days* — so you can move forward quickly with confidence.

  • Priority application handling
  • Direct university liaising
  • Offer letter tracking & follow-up
  • Conditional offer guidance
  • Acceptance & CAS management
💰
Education Loan at Any Cost

Financing your UK education shouldn't be a barrier. We work with leading Indian banks and NBFCs to secure education loans — with or without collateral — with high approval rates in just 4 days*.

  • Collateral & non-collateral loans
  • SBI, HDFC, Axis, NBFC partners
  • Loan amount up to ₹1.5 Cr
  • Hassle-free documentation
  • 4-day sanction process*
🛂
UK Student Visa Processing

Our visa team ensures your UK Tier 4 / Student Visa application is complete, correct and submitted on time — maximising your approval chances and minimising delays.

  • CAS & visa document checklist
  • IELTS / Duolingo guidance
  • Financial documents review
  • Visa application filing
  • Biometrics & VFS support
✈️
Pre-Departure & Settlement

Your journey doesn't end at visa approval. We prepare you for life in the UK — accommodation options, travel insurance, forex, banking, NHS registration and more.

  • Accommodation search & guidance
  • Forex & banking setup
  • Travel insurance recommendations
  • NHS registration support
  • UK arrival orientation
